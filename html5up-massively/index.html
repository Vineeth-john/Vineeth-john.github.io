Liver diseases are a significant health concern and a leading cause of mortality worldwide. Among the various liver diseases, liver tumors are particularly noteworthy. These tumors represent abnormal and uncontrolled growths of liver cells, which can develop on the surface or within the liver itself. Liver tumors can be benign or malignant, with the latter posing a serious threat due to its potential to spread to other parts of the body.
